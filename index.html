About Me

My name is Constantinos Iasonides. Passion. Creation. Work ethic. That’s the three most important words that I can relate to in my life. I am currently working on my Bachelor's Degree at Hanzehogeschool of Groningen in the Netherlands. As a child I liked to play with Legos, creating statues and buildings from scratch. Whatever I did, either swim, play with Lego, be in the Special Forces, I did it with passion. As I get older, Lego's are being replaced by gaming consoles. My Bachelor Degree is in Game Design and Development.

I am curious and Game Design excites me. When you start designing a project, you become excited and you don’t know where it is going to take you. At the end when you complete the work, the outcome is very fulfilling. What I mean is that enthusiasm comes when you meet your target and that’s why I want to be a Game designer. On the way to complete your target, you may get frustrated but working on it and trying to find a solution, you get to finish the product and that is very fulfilling.

I began my studies at Pascal English School in Larnaca, Cyprus. The courses that I had to attend were Mathematics, ICT, and Media. In Media courses I learned how to create magazines, manage Photoshop, create pictures, change layers and colours. In addition, ICT courses taught me how to use Microsoft Office but most importantly how technology evolved over the years. On one part curiosity is what makes me go on but also the feeling, after I have the finished product, the feeling is so exciting. Confusion and frustration is part of the game but that is only when you start working on something. Satisfaction and pleasure overcomes at the end.

I’m currently working on projects with UNITY engine, create illustrations and pictures in Illustrator and Photoshop, build websites in Java Script and create and design Logos.
