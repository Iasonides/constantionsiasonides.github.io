About Me

My name is Constantinos Iasonides. Passion. Creation. Work ethic. That’s the three most important words that I can relate with in my life. Until now I am currently working on finishing my bachelor's at Hanzehogeschool of Groningen in the Netherlands. My bachelor course is Game Design and Development.

I began my studies at Pascal English School in Larnaca, Cyprus. My studies that I had to attend to were Mathematics, ICT, and Media. In Media courses I learned how to create magazines, manage Photoshop, create pictures, change layers and colours. In addition to that ICT courses, taught me how to use Microsoft Office but most importantly how technology evolved over the years.

At the moment, I’m currently working on projects with Unity engine, create illustrations and pictures in Illustrator and Photoshop, build websites in Java Script and create and design logos.
